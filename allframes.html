All Frames Codex

Starter entries for a Warframe collection page. Add more cards as new frames are introduced.

Excalibur

Balanced Starter

  • Acquisition: Mars (War boss fight)
  • Focus: Melee burst + crowd control
  • Codex Notes: Great baseline frame to compare others against.

Volt

Speed / Utility

  • Acquisition: Clan Dojo (Tenno Lab)
  • Focus: Team speed, shield play, electric damage
  • Codex Notes: Common pick for fast farming and Eidolon roles.

Rhino

Tank / Team Buff

  • Acquisition: Venus (Fossa boss fight)
  • Focus: Survivability + damage buffing
  • Codex Notes: Beginner-friendly and useful all the way into endgame.
